Proposed Initiatives

(Any and all feedback is welcome and appreciated!)

Elevating the Academic Experience

  • Inside the classroom
    • Upholding and upgrading norms of classroom participation
    • Inviting distinguished guests
      • BYO(Boss) to Class Day
      • Parents Day, with remote dial-in for international parents
  • Outside the classroom
    • Peer tutors for study groups (especially for FRC)

Inclusive & Safe Socials

  • For the party animals
    • Ragers with responsibility (focus on harassment prevention with Women’s Rep)
  • For the homebodies
    • Regular programming of game nights, movie nights, wine nights with a dedicated social chair

Supporting Long-Term Career Success (Partners always invited!)

  • For the entrepreneurs
    • Section pitch days to practise product demos, fundraising pitch, etc.
  • For targeted job-seekers
    • Recurring focused 1-1 lunch pairings
    • Peer resume reviews
    • Network exchange Excel (“I am looking for xyz”, “I can connect you with x”)
    • Matching with EC buddy (with RC-EC Liaison)
    • Industry specific peer presentations to the section
  • For explorers (including interested sponsored students)
    • Recurring small group lunches, sometimes with ECs
    • “Day in the life of a consultant/PE bro/VC bro/product manager” presentations to the section
  • Long-term initiatives
    • Priority bids: Be the first investor in your section-mate’s new venture
    • Local support: Be the first point of contact for your section mate if they’re recruiting in or expanding their venture to your geography

Technology Beyond The Classroom

  • BLookUp: Develop basic tool for looking up sectionmates, what they’ve done and what they’re looking for
  • WhatsApp/Slack bot for reminders, scheduling, announcements, etc. (aspirational, but doable)

Embracing Cultural Exchange

  • Culture calendar with appointed culture carriers for respective geography/language/ethnic group
  • Potlucks!
  • “Teach me a word…” (everyone knows at least 1 word from each language in the section by end of RC year)
  • Potlucks!
  • Long-term initiatives
    • Authentic country trips
    • 5Y, 10Y reunions in US and non-US locations

Float Like a Butterfly, Sting Like a Bee

  • Health & wellness
    • Peer gym buddies – Help beginners get fit, show them proper form, spot them, push them, keep them accountable
    • Diet – WhatsApp group for healthy recipes and meal prep
  • Sports & Athletics
    • Keep sport-specific interest groups alive with self-appointed captains
    • 1 match-up a month vs another section (play and win against all sections by the end of RC)

Going Beyond Shareholder Value

  • Cerebral volunteering – Social responsibility projects that appeal to your intellectual capabilities
  • MyTakes - Share what matters most to you and why
