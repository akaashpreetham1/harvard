About Me

I’m Akaash Preetham. I’m running to represent you with a simple promise: listen first, act on evidence, and keep communication clear and transparent.

Background & Education

At Harvard, I’ve focused on connecting students with the resources they need to succeed—academically and personally. My academic work emphasizes rigorous, practical approaches to problem solving.

I value inclusive spaces where collaboration leads to better outcomes for everyone.

Experience & Leadership

  • Improved visibility into study space availability and usage
  • Built mentorship bridges between first-years and upperclass students
  • Partnered with faculty and admin to refine course feedback loops
  • Organized cross-house events to strengthen a welcoming community

Vision & Values

I believe no one should feel left behind. I’ll champion evidence-based solutions, open communication, and accountable, student-centered policy.
